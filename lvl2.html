Player vs Villain

Your Turn

Rules:

  1. The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
  2. You are X and the computer is O. You take turns putting your marks in empty squares.
  3. The first to get 3 marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) loses.
  4. When all 9 squares are full, the game ends in a tie if no player has 3 in a row.
